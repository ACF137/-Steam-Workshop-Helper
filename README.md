## What is this

A browser extension that adds a small panel to the Steam Workshop item editor.  
Instead of manually filling the same form fields every time you upload — just click a button.

The panel appears automatically when you open the upload page and stays out of the way the rest of the time.

---

## When does it work

Only activates on Steam Workshop upload pages:
```
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/edititem/*
```
On all other sites the extension does nothing.

---

## How to use

1. Open the Steam Workshop upload page
2. The **SteamKit** panel appears in the top-left corner of the page
3. Click the button you need — the form fields fill instantly
4. Upload your item as usual

The **−** button in the top-right of the panel collapses it if it gets in the way.

---

## Buttons

**🖼 ILLUSTRATIONS**  
Sets the image dimensions required to upload a GIF as a Steam illustration.

**🔧 WORKSHOP**  
Fills the app ID, file type and visibility fields required to upload to Workshop.
